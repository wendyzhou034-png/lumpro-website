Transparent LED Display for Retail Windows in the Philippines

Capture attention in Manila's premier shopping districts with futuristic glass-like LED technology that doesn't block the view.

Published: 2026-07-09

In the competitive retail landscape of Metro Manila, standing out in destinations like BGC and Makati requires a modern visual touch. In 2026, **Transparent LED Displays** have emerged as the ultimate solution for high-end retail windows.

What is a Transparent LED Display?

Designed to be mounted behind glass, these screens feature up to 85% transparency. When off, they appear as a tinted window; when on, they display vibrant, floating HD videos that attract foot traffic without blocking natural light.

Key Benefits for Retailers

  • High Transparency: Turn your window into a marketing asset without sacrificing store visibility.
  • Extreme Brightness: Up to 5500 nits to combat direct Manila sunlight.
  • Lightweight Design: Easy installation on glass facades without heavy structural support.

Technical Specs & GOB Protection

For retail windows, pixel pitches like **P3.91 - P7.82** provide the best balance of clarity and transparency. At **LumPro**, we apply **GOB (Glue-on-Board)** technology to our transparent series to protect the delicate diodes from dust and impact, ensuring a 10-year lifespan even in humid tropical climates.
