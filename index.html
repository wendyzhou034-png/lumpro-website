FAQ

Buyer questions

Answers for real buyers and search engines.

How to choose LED display pixel pitch?

Pixel pitch depends on viewing distance and application. Close-viewing indoor projects usually need finer pitch options, while outdoor or long-distance projects can use larger pixel pitch for better cost control.

What is the difference between indoor and outdoor LED display?

Indoor LED displays focus on close-viewing clarity and slim installation. Outdoor LED displays need higher brightness, weather resistance and stable performance in changing environments.

Do you support LED display projects in the Philippines?

Yes. LumProTech highlights local warehouse support in Manila, Cebu and Davao to help improve response, delivery coordination and project communication.

Can LED display size be customized?

Yes. Screen size, pixel pitch, cabinet type and installation method can be recommended according to the site, budget and viewing distance.

How fast can LumProTech provide a quotation?

What information is needed before ordering?
