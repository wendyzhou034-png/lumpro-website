How to Choose an LED Display Supplier in the Philippines

Compare more than price before selecting a supplier for your project.

Published: 2026-07-12

Choosing an LED display supplier in the Philippines affects product quality, installation efficiency and long-term maintenance. A clear comparison should include technical specifications, factory testing, project experience and after-sales response.

Start with a Project-Based Recommendation

A professional supplier should ask for screen size, indoor or outdoor use, viewing distance, installation location, content type and delivery date. Quoting a product without this information can lead to an unsuitable pixel pitch or cabinet design.

Compare Complete Specifications

Do not compare quotations by price alone. Review pixel pitch, module size, cabinet material, brightness, refresh rate, power consumption, control system, maintenance method and spare-parts quantity. Confirm what is included in the quotation.

Review Real Projects and Factory Process

Real project photos and videos help verify application experience. Factory information should show assembly, aging tests, calibration, inspection and packing rather than only promotional claims.

Consider Local Response

Local warehouse or service support can improve parts availability, communication and urgent project coordination. LumProTech supports Philippine customers through Manila, Cebu and Davao while providing factory-direct product configuration.

Request a Clear Proposal

Before ordering, ask for a screen layout, resolution calculation, power requirements, packing list and delivery plan. Clear documentation makes it easier for contractors, installers and end users to coordinate.
