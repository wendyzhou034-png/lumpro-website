FAQ

LED Display Questions

Practical answers for planning a commercial LED display project.

What information is needed for a quotation?

Please provide the screen width and height, installation location, indoor or outdoor use, viewing distance and target delivery date.

How do I choose pixel pitch?

A shorter viewing distance generally requires a smaller pixel pitch. We recommend the right option based on content, viewing distance and budget.

Are displays tested before shipment?

Yes. Orders follow module inspection, cabinet checks, power and signal testing, aging tests, calibration and final review.

Do you support projects in the Philippines?

We provide warehouse and service support for Manila, Cebu and Davao.
