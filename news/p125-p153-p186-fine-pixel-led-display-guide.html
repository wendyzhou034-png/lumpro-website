P1.25 vs P1.53 vs P1.86 Fine Pixel LED Display

How to balance close-viewing clarity, screen size and project budget.

Published: 2026-07-12

Fine pixel LED displays are widely used in boardrooms, control centers, studios, command rooms and premium showrooms. The correct pitch depends on the nearest viewing distance, the amount of fine text shown and the total project budget.

P1.25: Maximum Detail

P1.25 provides the highest pixel density of these three options. It is suitable for executive rooms, broadcast environments and data-heavy applications where viewers sit close to the screen. It also supports high-resolution content on a relatively compact wall.

P1.53: Balanced High Resolution

P1.53 offers an effective balance between visual detail and investment. It works well for conference rooms, monitoring centers and presentation spaces that need sharp text and smooth video without moving to the smallest pitch.

P1.86: Strong Commercial Value

P1.86 is a popular choice for larger meeting rooms, commercial halls and showrooms. At a suitable viewing distance it delivers clear content while controlling system cost. It is often the best starting point for buyers comparing fine-pixel solutions.

Other Specifications Matter

Pixel pitch is only one factor. Refresh rate, grayscale, color calibration, cabinet flatness, service access and control-system compatibility all affect the finished display. LumProTech can calculate the screen resolution and cabinet layout after receiving your target screen size.
