Trifold & Creative Mobile LED Display Series: Portability Redefined

Learn how our folding modular design is solving logistics challenges for mall activations and corporate seminars in the Philippines.

Published: 2026-07-03

In 2026, the demand for fast-deployment visual solutions has skyrocketed across Metro Manila, Cebu, and Davao. Traditional fixed installations aren't always the best fit for dynamic retail environments. This is where **LumPro's Trifold & Creative Mobile LED Series** comes in.

Ultra-Portability Meets High Performance

The Trifold series features a unique modular design where the screen folds into three sections, allowing it to fit through standard service elevators and commercial doors in BGC or Makati malls. Within minutes, the screen unfolds and powers on, eliminating complex mounting needs.

Best Applications for Mobile LED

  • Luxury Retail & Pop-ups: High-impact digital standees that can be moved based on daily foot traffic patterns.
  • Corporate Seminars: Reducing setup time and trucking costs for city-to-city HR orientations.
  • VIP Events: High-resolution welcoming signs for luxury weddings and gala receptions.

The LumPro GOB Advantage

Because mobile units are handled frequently, we use **GOB (Glue-on-Board)** technology to make modules impact-resistant, ensuring zero dead pixels during transport across the Philippine islands.

Supported by local technical teams in **Manila, Cebu, and Davao**, LumPro ensures your mobile display is always calibrated and ready for action.
